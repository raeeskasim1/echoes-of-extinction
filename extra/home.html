Discover animals that once ruled the Earth but disappeared forever

Extinction is the permanent disappearance of a species from Earth. Some animals vanished because of climate change, hunting, habitat loss, or human activity. This website explores lost species and animals still fighting for survival.

Woolly Mammoth

The Woolly Mammoth was a large Ice Age animal related to modern elephants. It had thick fur, long curved tusks, and lived in cold regions. It became extinct thousands of years ago due to climate change and human hunting.
