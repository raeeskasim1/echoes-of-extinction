What is Extinction?

Extinction happens when the last living member of a species dies. Once a species disappears forever, it cannot naturally return. Some extinctions happen slowly over thousands of years, while others happen quickly because of human actions.

Why Do Species Become Extinct?

Habitat Loss

When forests and natural homes are destroyed, animals lose the space they need to live.

Hunting

Some species disappeared because humans hunted them too much.

Climate Change

Changing weather and temperatures can make habitats unsafe for many species.

Pollution

Pollution damages land, water, and air, making survival harder for wildlife.
